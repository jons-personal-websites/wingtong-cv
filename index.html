About

Designing with intent

I'm a Senior Product Designer based in Singapore with over a decade of experience spanning F&B technology, FinTech, and digital transformation consultancy. I specialize in end-to-end product development — from user research and wireframing through to high-fidelity prototypes and design system governance.

At A5tec, I led Design Ops and educated cross-functional teams on Figma best practices. At EY, I facilitated digital transformation for strategic accounts across industries. My Fine Arts background from NTU, with a focus on Interactive Media, gives me a unique lens on both the aesthetic and functional dimensions of product design.
